Curious Minds Ask

What ages can participate in KidzCamp?

KidzCamp is designed for children aged 6-12 years old who are eager to unleash their inner superhero!

How are the activities structured at KidzCamp?

Our activities are carefully curated to blend excitement with skill-building, ensuring a dynamic and enriching experience for every child.

Are meals provided at KidzCamp?

Yes, we offer nutritious and delicious meals to fuel your child's adventures throughout the day at KidzCamp.

What safety measures are in place at KidzCamp?

Safety is our top priority! We have trained staff, secure facilities, and strict protocols to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all campers.

How can I enroll my child in KidzCamp?

Enrolling your child is quick and easy! Simply click the 'Join Now' button, fill out the form, and get ready for an unforgettable adventure at KidzCamp!
